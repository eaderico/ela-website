About Mrs. Derico:

My name is Elizabeth Derico, and I love teaching English Language Arts! This year is my 6th year of teaching. My favorite hobby is reading, and I love helping students develop a love of reading as well. I like to keep track of how many books I read each year, so this year we are going to have a classroom reading challenge! More details about this will come soon.
On a personal note, I am married and have five children, ages 19 (twins), 17, 14, and 12. My husband is the director of a summer camp, so I spend my summer volunteering there (and also reading, of course!). We love spending time outdoors together.
